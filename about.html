About College
Milestone

College Milestone, a bilingual private college on the south shore of Montreal, is an institution authorized by the Ministry
of Education to offer ACA (Attestation of Collegial Studies) and DVS (Diplomas of Vocational Studies) programs.

We welcome you
to College Milestone!

You will find with us a living environment where knowledge is communicated by teachers who are passionate, competent, and committed to education. Added to this is a team of professionals concerned with the development of the full potential of each student.

In the era of new technologies and digital technology, Collège Milestone aims to be an environment where students have access to innovative training programs.

Choosing our college also means choosing a college with human values that promotes academic excellence among our students, while giving them the opportunity to enrich themselves through contact with others and to flourish in as human beings.

I invite you to browse our website to discover all the services, technical collegial programs (Attestation of Collegial Studies – ACS) and professional training programs (Diploma of Vocational Studies – DVS) that we offer.

Looking forward to welcoming you into our big family!

Manon Delisle

General Director

Our Mission

● An educational institution committed to provide quality professional and technical programs in both official languages in Québec.

● An educational institution with significant knowledge and experience in international education committed to helping Québec become a destination of choice for international students.

● An institution committed to the betterment of the Québec education system.

● An institution devoted to the successful graduation of skilled individuals to meet the employment needs of Québec employers.

At Collège Milestone we bring to life five values:

  • Respect: at the center of our interactions
  • Commitment: for student success and perseverance
  • Integrity: in all our practices
  • Innovation & Creativity: to carry out our projects
  • Collaboration: enriching each other
